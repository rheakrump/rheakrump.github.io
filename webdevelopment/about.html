Who am I?

I’m from Marceline, a small town in north central Missouri.

Currently, I am a second-year English major studying at the University of Missouri, Columbia. My emphasis area is Creative Writing. I intend to enter the career field of writing and editing upon graduation in May 2026.

I have had the opportunity to explore my interest in writing through personal and academic endeavors. One of my most recent personal projects is the beginnings of a novel. I am also working on a blog to share specific academic writings and thoughts. Some of my hobbies include thrift-flipping clothes, sewing, and watercolor painting. I also enjoy hiking and anything outdoorsy.
An “about me” wouldn’t be complete without mentioning my family and friends. I try to make my relationships with the people in my life my first priority.
