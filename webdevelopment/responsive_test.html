Hello!

My name is Rhea Krumpelman, welcome to my writing portfolio!

I have always been captivated by good writing and an author’s ability to bring stories and ideas to life on the page, hence my decision to become an English major.

Here, you will find a selection of my writing. The works range from thought provoking essays to poetry written for a college course. I believe that range is one of the greatest skills a writer can possess. To learn versatility, it is beneficial to be well-rounded so that one’s interests encompass a variety of topics. On the “About Me” page, I show how having broad interests has impacted my writing.

Feel free to browse my works, leave comments, and reach out if something sparks your interest!
